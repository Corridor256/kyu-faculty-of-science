About Me

I am Kavuma Rodgers, a passionate and driven second-year student at Kyambogo University, pursuing a Bachelor of Science with Education in Chemistry and Mathematics. Born on 24th July 2004 in Kalagala village, Lwengo sub-county, Lwengo county, Lwengo district, Masaka, Uganda — my journey from a small village in Lwengo to the halls of one of Uganda's leading universities is a testament to resilience, curiosity, and an unrelenting desire to learn.

Beyond the classroom, I have a deep-rooted love for computing, software, and development. This passion pushed me into the world of web development, where I am actively learning and building. This very website — the Faculty of Science Student Portal — is my first published project, and it represents the beginning of what I envision as a long and impactful career at the intersection of science and technology.

I dream of becoming a Computational Chemist who leverages Artificial Intelligence and mathematical modeling to revolutionize chemical research, making the microscopic world more accessible, visual, and actionable for researchers worldwide.

Our Mission

To provide quality science education, conduct impactful research, and produce competent graduates who contribute to national development and the advancement of scientific knowledge in Uganda and beyond.

Our Vision

To be a leading Faculty of Science in the region, recognized for academic excellence, innovative research, and the production of highly skilled science educators and professionals.

Core Values

Excellence, Integrity, Innovation, Teamwork, Inclusivity, and a commitment to service and the pursuit of knowledge for the betterment of society.

Our History & Overview

The Faculty of Science at Kyambogo University was established following the merger of the Uganda Polytechnic Kyambogo (UPK), the Institute of Teacher Education Kyambogo (ITEK), and the Uganda National Institute of Special Education (UNISE) in 2003 to form Kyambogo University.

Since its inception, the Faculty has grown into a vibrant academic community dedicated to training science educators and researchers. With multiple departments covering the core scientific disciplines, we offer a range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs designed to equip students with both theoretical knowledge and practical skills.

Our graduates serve in secondary schools, research institutions, industries, and government agencies across East Africa and beyond, carrying forward the tradition of excellence that defines Kyambogo University.

Our Departments

Department of Chemistry

Offers programs in analytical, organic, inorganic, and physical chemistry. Focuses on training chemistry educators and conducting research in applied chemistry.

  • Modern chemistry laboratories
  • Research in environmental chemistry
  • Industrial chemistry partnerships

Department of Physics

Covers mechanics, electromagnetism, nuclear physics, electronics, and physics education. Equipped with practical labs for hands-on learning.

  • Electronics & digital lab
  • Nuclear physics research
  • Renewable energy projects

Department of Biology

Specializes in genetics, ecology, evolution, animal physiology, and biology education. Strong emphasis on field work and conservation studies.

  • Genetics & molecular biology lab
  • Biodiversity research
  • Ecology field stations

Department of Mathematics

Teaches pure and applied mathematics, statistics, numerical analysis, and mathematics education. Prepares students for teaching and analytical careers.

  • Computer mathematics lab
  • Statistics & data science
  • Mathematics education research
